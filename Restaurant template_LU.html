Services

1. Dine-in
Customers can enjoy their meals in the restaurant.
2.Takeaway
Customers can order food to be packed and taken away.
3. Delivery
Food is delivered to customers' homes or offices
4. Catering
The restaurant provides food and service for special events and gatherings.
5. Buffet
Customers can choose from a variety of dishes displayed in a self-service format.

Products

Spices
A wide range of Indian spices are available for purchase, including turmeric, cumin, coriander, cardamom, and more.
Condiments
Popular condiments like chutneys, pickles, and relishes are often sold separately.
Ready-to-eat Meals
Pre-packaged Indian meals that can be heated and consumed at home.
Snacks
Items like samosas, pakoras, and papadums are often sold as snacks.
Sweets
Traditional Indian sweets like gulab jamun, jalebi, and barfi are popular products.

Dishes

  • Biryani
    A flavorful rice dish cooked with aromatic spices, meat (chicken, mutton, or seafood), and sometimes vegetables.
  • Butter Chicken
    A creamy and mildly spiced chicken dish cooked in a tomato and butter-based sauce.
  • Tandoori Chicken
    Chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, cooked in a traditional clay oven called a tandoor.
  • Palak Paneer
    A vegetarian dish made with spinach and cottage cheese (paneer) cooked in a spiced gravy.
  • Masala Dosa
  • Rogan Josh
  • Chole Bhature
  • Pani Puri
  • Vada Pav
  • Gulab Jamun
